1. Product Definition

The NOCO Boost Plus GB40 is a compact, portable 12-volt lithium-ion jump starter rated at 1,000 amps of peak current. It is designed to safely jump start dead lead-acid vehicle batteries in seconds and also functions as a USB power bank and LED emergency flashlight.

2. Who This Product Is For

  • Drivers who want an emergency jump starter in the car for dead batteries.
  • Owners of gasoline vehicles up to 6.0L or diesel vehicles up to 3.0L displacement.
  • People who want a portable power bank to recharge phones and small USB devices while traveling.
  • Users who need a compact jump starter for cars, SUVs, trucks, boats, ATVs, and lawn equipment with 12V batteries.

7. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What types of vehicles can this jump starter be used on?

A: It is designed for 12V lead-acid batteries in most cars, SUVs, light trucks, boats, motorcycles, ATVs, lawn tractors, and similar equipment, within the general guideline of up to 6.0L gasoline and 3.0L diesel engines.

Q: How many jump starts can it provide on a single charge?

A: Under typical conditions, the internal battery is rated to provide multiple jump starts on a full charge. Actual results depend on battery condition, temperature, and engine size.

Q: Can it charge my phone or tablet?

A: Yes. The GB40 has a USB output port that can power smartphones, tablets, and other small USB devices within the limits of its internal capacity.

Q: How long does it take to recharge the GB40 itself?

A: When connected to a suitable USB power source, a full recharge typically takes a few hours. Exact time depends on the output of the charger being used.

Q: Is it a battery maintainer or charger?

A: No. The GB40 is intended for short-term jump starting and as a portable power bank. It is not a replacement for a dedicated battery charger or maintainer.
